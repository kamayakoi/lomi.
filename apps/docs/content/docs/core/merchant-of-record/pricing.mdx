All transactions on lomi. come with a flat fee of **2.9% + 200 XOF | 2.9% + $0.3** applied to the entire transaction amount. There are no other transaction fees — no subscription charges, no premium fees for WhatsApp communication or emails, and no extra charges for regular payouts.

- **Dynamic (volume-tiered):** rates decrease automatically as your monthly processed volume grows.
- **Fixed:** a stable fee schedule that does not change with monthly volume.
- **Custom:** negotiated pricing for specific business models, risk profiles, or high-volume needs.

The exact fee values visible to your organization come from your active pricing configuration in lomi. and may differ by currency, payment method, and provider constraints.

## How dynamic pricing works

With dynamic pricing, your organization is assigned to a tier based on processed volume for the period. As your volume increases, your transaction rates decrease automatically.

- Tiers are evaluated from your processed revenue.
- Tiered fees apply to core payment processing fees (for example Mobile Money and cards).
- Non-tier fees can still apply (for example refunds, disputes, international card surcharges, or subscription-related add-ons).

For current tier thresholds and live rates, use the pricing view in your dashboard or the public pricing page at [lomi.africa/pricing](https://lomi.africa/pricing).

## Fixed and custom pricing

Not every business should be on volume-tiered pricing:

- **Fixed** is useful when you want predictable fees for finance planning.
- **Custom** is useful when your payment mix, ticket size, geography, or compliance setup needs a tailored schedule.

Custom pricing can include bespoke combinations of percentage and fixed components, and can vary by payment method and currency.

| **Item**                                                 | **Amount** |
| :------------------------------------------------------- | :--------- |
| Original Transaction Value                               | 30,000 XOF |
| Transaction Fee (2.9% + 200 XOF)                         | 930 XOF    |
| Refund Processing Fee (4% — charged to merchant)         | 1,200 XOF  |
| Final Refund Amount to Customer (in case of full refund) | 30,000 XOF |

&nbsp;

<Callout type="info">
  lomi. reserves the right to issue refunds at our discretion within 60 days of

  **$15 (10,000 XOF)** per dispute, charged directly to the merchant. We only
  exercise this right to help minimize chargebacks, avoid unexpected charges,
  and reduce associated fees on your behalf.
</Callout>

Transaction fees are calculated from:

`transaction fee = (percentage x amount) + fixed amount`

Depending on your pricing mode and method, the fee can include:

**Important:**

- Payment providers impose monitoring programs, penalties, and higher chargeback costs for sellers with high chargeback rates (~0.7%+).
- We monitor and manage chargeback rates to prevent exceeding problematic thresholds.
- If chargebacks increase, we may need to intervene or suspend accounts unless proactive measures are taken.

In some low-amount flows, only the percentage component may apply based on your organization fee configuration.

## Additional fees

Some fees are scenario-specific rather than charged on every payment:

- **international card surcharge** (when applicable)
- **subscription payment add-on** (when applicable)
- **refund processing fee** (full or partial refunds)
- **chargeback/dispute fee** (set by underlying networks/providers)

These are applied only when the relevant event occurs.

| **Method**                  | **Fee**                   | **Processing Time** |
| :-------------------------- | :------------------------ | :------------------ |
| International Bank Transfer | 1% + 3,500 XOF (or $5.00) | 1-2 business days   |
| Local Bank Transfer         | Free                      | 1-2 business days   |
| Mobile Money                | Free \| 1%                | Instant             |

&nbsp;

<Callout type="info">
  The 1% fee for a Mobile Money payouts is **not** a lomi. fee. It is a standard
  fee charged directly by mobile money operators during the payout process,
  which lomi. simply passes through.
</Callout>

- Original processing fees are generally non-refundable because providers charge them at payment time.
- Refund-specific fees may apply depending on your active fee schedule.
- Card disputes/chargebacks are billed by underlying networks and passed through.
